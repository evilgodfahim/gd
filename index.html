Guardian Unified RSS Feed

This repository publishes a single merged RSS 2.0 feed built from multiple Guardian sources. The feed updates automatically every 10 minutes and enforces strict deduplication, time filtering, and category exclusion.

RSS feed URL

merged.xml

Compatible with Inoreader and any standard RSS reader.

Articles older than 48 hours are excluded. Maximum capacity is 1000 items, maintained in circular order with newest items at the top.
